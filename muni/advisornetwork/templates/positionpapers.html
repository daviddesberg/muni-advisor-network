What is a Position Paper?

Simply put, a position paper is used to express your views to your fellow delegates before the conference begins. A position paper outlines the views of your home government on the topics that will be debated in your committee. A good position paper includes a brief description of the situation at a global level, your home government’s official position on the topic, and policies or actions that your home government would support for addressing the topic. Position Papers are due Friday, March 3 by 11:59pm.

The specifications for position papers are listed below:
  • Maximum one (1) page per topic
  • Times New Roman 12pt font
  • Single Spaced
  • 1" margins

  • Illinois General Assembly

*please submit a position paper for only two of the topic areas
