Hello!

Welcome to the registration section of our website! We are glad you would like to attend the 22nd session of Model United Nations at Illinois!

Below you will find links to our Registration Guidebook which contains our detailed Financial Policy and Registration Process. You will also find a link to our Delegate Liability/Photo Release Forms. Please read these forms very carefully before moving on to the Registration Form below. All information regarding our Financial Policy and Registration Process, as well as the Delegate Liability/Photo Release Forms is final and no exception will be made to these policies.

Our conference and this registration system have been undergoing some exciting changes with the end goal of making sure you and your delegates have a stress free and enjoyable time preparing for and attending MUNI XXII. My goal as Under Secretary General of Registration is to be here for you every step of the way to assure a stress free registration experience and answer any and all questions you have regarding MUNI XXII.

Please note that the Advisor Network system is new to MUNI this year and new elements of it will become available within the coming weeks.

I look forward to working with you to make sure you and your delegates get the most out of your MUNI experience this year! Please do not hesitate to contact me at any time with all of your questions, comments, and concerns at registration@illinoismun.org.

Best,
John Hall
Under Secretary General of Registration | MUNI XXII
