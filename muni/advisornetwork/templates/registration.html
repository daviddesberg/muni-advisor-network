Registration


Hello!

Welcome to the registration page of MUNI XXIII. We are very happy that you are considering our conference.

Below you can find the MUNI XXIII Registration Table which contains all of the important dates pertaining to registration periods and payment deadlines as well as all of the fees and their amounts for our conference. You will also find links to both our Registration Guidebook, which contains our detailed Financial Policy and Registration Process, as well as our Delegate Liability/Photo Release Forms. The policies outlined in the Registration Guidebook and the Delegate Liability/Photo Release Forms are final and very important.

We have kept most of the policies consistent from MUNI XXII, with the exception of small changes to the delegate drop fee. For schools that register during the regular registration period, the delegate drop fee has increased to $27.50. For schools that register during the late registration period, the delegate drop fee has increased to $30.00. Also, we are waiving the school fee for schools that will attend MUNI for the first time, or schools that have not attended MUNI for the last 5 years.

If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact me at registration@illinoismun.org! I look forward working with all of you to make this an enjoyable experience for you and your delegates.

Best,
Daniel Benson
Under Secretary General of Registration | MUNI XXIII
